Rules:


  • The name of the game is "Pig".
  • To begin, input the player names and press Start.
  • Player 1 always starts first.
  • The player rolls the die repeatedly to accumulate round points.
  • If the player rolls a 1, they will lose all of their round points on that turn and the turn will pass to the other player.
  • If the player holds, all the round points they accumulated at that turn will be added to their total score.
  • If a player reaches 50 total points, they win and the game is over.
  • If the player reaches 50 current round points they automatically win, even without holding.
  • A player can skip their turn by pressing Hold even without rolling the die.
